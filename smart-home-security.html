Advanced & Smart Home Security

Modernize your home protection with cutting-edge smart lock technology.

The future of home security is here, and it offers more convenience and control than ever before. At CCR Locksmith, we specialize in the installation and integration of smart home security systems. From keyless smart locks to video doorbells and remote access controls, we help you build a connected home that keeps you safe and informed, no matter where you are.

Why Upgrade to Smart Home Security?

Smart security devices provide layers of protection that traditional locks cannot match. Here are some of the key benefits:

  • Remote Access: Lock or unlock your door from anywhere using your smartphone.
  • Activity Monitoring: Receive real-time alerts when someone enters or leaves your home.
  • Keyless Convenience: No more fumbling for keys or worrying about lost copies. Use codes, fingerprints, or your phone.
  • Guest Management: Create temporary access codes for guests, dog walkers, or service providers.
  • Integration: Seamlessly connect with other smart home devices like cameras and alarm systems.

Our Smart Security Services

We offer a range of services to bring your home into the modern era of security:

Smart Lock Installation

We install top-rated smart locks from leading brands like August, Schlage, Yale, and Kwikset. Whether you prefer a retrofit option that works with your existing deadbolt or a complete replacement, we ensure a perfect fit and secure setup.

Video Doorbell Setup

See who is at your door before you open it. We install video doorbells that allow you to see, hear, and speak to visitors from your phone, adding a powerful layer of security to your front entrance.

System Integration & Configuration

Technology can be tricky. Our technicians handle the technical setup, connecting your devices to your Wi-Fi network, configuring user accounts, and teaching you how to use the app features effectively.

Professional Installation Matters

While many smart devices are marketed as DIY, improper installation can leave your home vulnerable or lead to connectivity issues. Our professional locksmiths ensure that the physical lock is installed correctly for maximum strength and that the digital components are configured securely to prevent unauthorized access.
