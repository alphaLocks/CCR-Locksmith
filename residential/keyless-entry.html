Install & Setup Process

  1. Assess & Quote: Confirm door compatibility and provide upfront pricing.
  2. Install & Reinforce: Fit hardware, align latch, reinforce strike with 3" screws.
  3. Program: Set master/admin code, add user/guest codes, connect apps if smart-enabled.
  4. Training & Warranty: Show backups (key/9V), test with you, 5-Year Warranty on parts & labor.

Why Upgrade to Keyless Entry?

  • No more lost keys—share temporary codes for guests or contractors
  • Change codes anytime; some smart locks allow schedules and activity logs
  • Secure hardware with proper installation improves door strength
  • Most models keep working during power outages (battery operated)

FAQs

Do you provide the lock or can you install one I bought?

Either. We can supply recommended models or install a homeowner-provided lock after verifying compatibility.

Can I keep a physical key as backup?

Yes. Most keypad and smart deadbolts include a keyed cylinder and/or a 9V emergency jump option.

Will a keypad fit my existing deadbolt hole?

Most modern units fit standard 2-1/8" bore holes. If needed, we adjust the door and reinforce the strike for best security.

Can you integrate with my phone or voice assistant?

Many smart locks support mobile apps and popular voice assistants. We’ll pair and test during installation if the model supports it.
