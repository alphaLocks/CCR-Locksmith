What We Do

  • Safe Opening & Lockout: Non-destructive methods first; precision drilling only if required, followed by full restoration.
  • Installation & Bolting: Delivery, placement, anchoring to concrete or framing with appropriate hardware.
  • Combination Changes: Mechanical dial changes and electronic keypad reprogramming, including multi-user codes.
  • Safe Lock Repair/Upgrade: Service or replace failed keypads, dials, and lock bodies; convert to electronic or mechanical as preferred.
  • Gun Safe Services: Opening, bolting, and maintenance for residential gun safes.
  • Fire/Burglary Protection Guidance: Advice on ratings and placement to protect documents, valuables, and firearms.

We verify ID/ownership before any opening or combination work — your security comes first.

Our Process

  1. Verify & Quote: Confirm identity/ownership, review safe type and symptoms, provide clear pricing.
  2. Assess Safe: Identify mechanical vs. electronic, relockers, and construction to choose the least invasive method.
  3. Open & Service: Manipulation/decoding first; precision entry if needed; then repair and service the lock.
  4. Secure & Bolt: Position and anchor the safe to resist removal; tidy finish work.
  5. Program & Train: Change combos, set user codes, and review best practices for code management.

FAQs

Can you open my home safe without damage?

We prioritize non-destructive methods. If drilling is the only option, we use precision techniques and restore the safe afterward.

Do you bolt safes to the floor?

Yes — concrete anchors or structural fastening into joists/studs, depending on location and safe size.

What if my keypad is dead?

We diagnose electronics, replace batteries or keypads, service the lock, or convert to a different lock type if needed.

Can you change my safe’s factory combination?

Yes, and we can add/remove user codes on electronic locks and document settings for you.

What brands do you handle?

We service most residential safe types — fire, burglary, gun, wall/floor — mechanical or electronic. Call with your model for specifics.
