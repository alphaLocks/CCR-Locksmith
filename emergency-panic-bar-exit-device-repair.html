Emergency Panic Bar & Exit Device Repair

Immediate response for critical exit door failures.

When an exit device fails, it creates an immediate security risk or a life safety hazard. If a door won't lock, your business is vulnerable. If it won't open, you are violating fire codes. CCR Locksmith provides 24/7 emergency repair services to address these critical issues instantly.

We Fix Urgent Issues Like:

  • Door Won't Latch: Leaving your building unsecured after hours.
  • Door Won't Open: Trapping occupants or blocking emergency egress.
  • Alarm Malfunction: Alarms sounding continuously or failing to arm.
  • Physical Damage: Broken bars or latches from break-in attempts or heavy use.

Why Call Us?

Our mobile units are stocked with common parts to perform on-the-spot repairs. If a full replacement is needed and parts aren't immediately available, we will secure the door and ensure the opening is safe until a permanent fix can be implemented.
