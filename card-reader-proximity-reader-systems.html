Efficient Access Management

Card reader and proximity reader systems are the standard for modern business security. They allow employees to enter authorized areas with a simple tap of a card or fob, eliminating the need for traditional keys. CCR Locksmith installs robust card access systems that are scalable and easy to administer.

Whether you need to secure a single server room or an entire corporate campus, we provide systems that give you full control. You can easily issue new cards, deactivate lost ones, and track entry logs, ensuring your facility remains secure at all times.

Our Card Reader Solutions:

  • Proximity Card Readers: Contactless entry for high-traffic areas.
  • Smart Card Systems: Encrypted cards for higher security applications.
  • Key Fob Systems: Convenient, durable alternatives to cards.
  • Long-Range Readers: Ideal for parking garages and vehicle gates.
  • Mobile Credentials: Use smartphones as access cards via Bluetooth/NFC.

Why Switch to Card Access?

  • Cost-Effective: Cheaper to replace a card than rekey a lock.
  • Granular Control: Restrict access by time, day, or user level.
  • Durability: Readers have no moving parts, reducing wear and tear.
  • Integration: Works seamlessly with alarm and time-attendance systems.
