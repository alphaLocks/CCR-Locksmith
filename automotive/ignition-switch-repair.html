Ignition Switch Repair in Metro Detroit

Key won’t turn, ignition jammed, or cylinder loose? We come to you.

CCR Locksmith diagnoses and repairs ignition switch and cylinder problems for most makes and models. In many cases we can repair the existing ignition instead of replacing the whole unit, saving you time and money.

Call Now: (313) 889-3266

Symptoms of a Failing Ignition

  • Key won’t insert or won’t turn
  • Key turns but engine won’t start
  • Stuck or locked cylinder; steering wheel frees but ignition doesn’t
  • Intermittent starting after wiggling the key
  • Visible damage after attempted theft

Common Causes We Repair

  • Worn keys and ignition wafers/tumblers
  • Broken springs or internal debris
  • Contact wear in the electrical switch
  • Weather-related binding or contamination
  • Theft or vandalism damage

Our Mobile Repair Process

  1. On-site diagnosis at your home, workplace, or roadside
  2. Non-destructive disassembly of the cylinder/switch as needed
  3. Refit/repair wafers, springs, or switch contacts; lubricate and reassemble
  4. Test with your key, or cut/program a fresh key if wear is severe
  5. If repair isn’t practical, we pivot to Ignition Switch Replacement

FAQs

Can you fix my ignition without replacing it?

Often yes. Many failures are caused by worn wafers/springs or a damaged tumbler; we aim to repair first. If repair isn’t possible, we’ll quote replacement on the spot.

Do you work on my brand?

We service ignitions across many brands. If special parts are required, we can source them quickly and complete the job on-site.
