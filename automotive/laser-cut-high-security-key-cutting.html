Sidewinder/laser keys cut and programmed on-site — no dealer visit.

Laser-cut keys (sidewinder) use center-milled, dual-sided grooves and often include a transponder or smart system. We bring dealer-level cutters and programmers to you and complete the job in one visit anywhere in Metro Detroit.

Cut & Program Process

  1. Verify & Quote: Confirm VIN/ownership and provide an upfront estimate.

FAQs

How do I know if my key is laser-cut?

Laser keys are thicker with a center groove and symmetrical edges; common on newer and luxury models.

Will I need programming after the cut?

Often yes. Many laser-cut keys include a transponder or work with a proximity system; we cut and program on-site.

Can you make a key if I lost all keys?

Yes. We can originate, cut, and program a new key/fob on-site across Metro Detroit.

Is this faster or cheaper than a dealership?

Typically faster and more convenient since we come to you, with competitive pricing.
