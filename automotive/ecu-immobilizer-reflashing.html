What Is ECU / Immobilizer Reflashing?

Reflashing is a controlled software procedure that resets or reprograms the vehicle’s ECU or immobilizer so it accepts new keys/fobs and correctly authorizes starting. It’s typically required when all keys are lost, a used ECU is installed, or the immobilizer becomes desynchronized.

  • Authorize brand‑new keys/fobs to start the vehicle
  • Resolve “key not recognized” and constant security lights
  • Support for push‑to‑start, smart key, and transponder systems

ECU & Immobilizer Reflashing — FAQs

Do I need to tow the car to a dealer?

No. We perform reflashing on‑site with mobile diagnostic equipment, saving you towing and dealership delays.

Will my old keys work after reflashing?

If all keys were lost, we typically authorize new keys and can disable missing keys for security.

How long does the job take?

Most vehicles are completed the same visit once we verify model, immobilizer type, and any required precoding.
