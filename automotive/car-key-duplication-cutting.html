Car Key Duplication & Cutting — Metro Detroit

Fast, damage-free key cutting and programming. 24/7 mobile service — we come to you.

We duplicate and cut car keys for most makes and models: standard metal keys, transponder/chipped keys, laser/sidewinder high-security keys, and smart/proximity fobs. Our mobile locksmiths serve Detroit, Southfield, Troy, Novi, Royal Oak, Warren, and the wider Metro Detroit area.

What We Duplicate

  • Standard metal car keys (non-transponder)
  • Transponder/chipped keys — cut & program
  • Laser/sidewinder high-security keys
  • Smart keys and proximity fobs
  • Key shells and worn key restorations

How We Do It

We use professional key machines, OEM-grade key blanks, and dealer-level programming tools. If you don’t have a working key, we can often cut by code or originate from the lock/ignition and then program the immobilizer on-site — saving a dealer tow.

Winter note: Michigan cold can make locks tight and keys brittle. We carry de-icing and lube protocols to protect your locks during service.

FAQs

Can you duplicate transponder and smart keys?

Yes. We cut and program most transponder and smart/proximity keys on-site for domestic and many foreign makes.

Do you cut laser/sidewinder high-security keys?

Yes. We carry laser cutting equipment and high-security blanks for sidewinder profiles common on newer vehicles.

Do I need my original key?

A working key helps, but if it’s lost or worn we can often originate a key by code or decode the lock, then program it.

How long does duplication take?

Simple duplicates take 10–20 minutes. Laser-cut and smart keys may take longer due to programming steps.
