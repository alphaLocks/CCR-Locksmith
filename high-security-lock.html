What Are High-Security Locks?

Standard locks can often be picked, bumped, or drilled in under a minute. High-security locks are engineered to resist these attacks. They feature complex cylinder mechanisms, hardened steel inserts, and restricted keyways. CCR Locksmith is your local expert for installing and servicing these advanced security solutions.

Benefits of High-Security Locks

  • Pick & Bump Resistance: Advanced pin configurations (like sidebars or telescopic pins) make manipulation nearly impossible.
  • Drill Resistance: Hardened steel inserts protect the cylinder from being drilled out.
  • Key Control: Patented keys cannot be duplicated at a standard hardware store. Only authorized individuals can get copies.
  • Durability: Built with heavier, stronger materials to withstand physical force and wear.

Brands We Work With

We install and service top-tier high-security brands, including:

  • Mul-T-Lock: Known for their interactive key systems and extreme durability.
  • Medeco: A leader in high-security cylinders with patented key control.
  • Schlage Primus: High-security upgrades for standard Schlage hardware.
  • Assa Abloy: Global leader in door opening solutions.
