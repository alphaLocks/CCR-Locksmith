High-Security Lock Installation

Maximum protection against picking, drilling, and bumping.

Standard locks can often be compromised by determined intruders using techniques like lock picking, bumping, or drilling. High-security locks are engineered to resist these attacks, providing a superior level of protection for your home or business. CCR Locksmith installs top-rated high-security hardware from trusted manufacturers.

What Makes a Lock "High-Security"?

  • Pick Resistance: Complex cylinder designs make them extremely difficult to manipulate.
  • Drill Resistance: Hardened steel inserts prevent drilling through the lock mechanism.
  • Bump Proof: Designed to be immune to "bump keys," a common method of break-in.
  • Key Control: Patented keyways ensure that keys cannot be duplicated without your authorization.

Our High-Security Solutions

We offer a range of high-security options tailored to your needs:

  • Mul-T-Lock & Medeco: Industry leaders in high-security cylinders and deadbolts.
  • Grade 1 Hardware: The highest ANSI grade for commercial and residential security.
  • Reinforced Strike Plates: Strengthening the door frame to prevent kick-ins.
