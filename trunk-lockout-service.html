Trunk Lockout Service

Fast and safe retrieval of keys locked in your trunk.

It happens in an instant—you put your groceries or gym bag in the trunk, close the lid, and realize your keys are still inside. Modern cars often have high-security trunks that cannot be opened from the inside of the cabin if the car is locked. CCR Locksmith provides specialized trunk lockout services to get your keys back without damaging your vehicle.

How We Open Locked Trunks

  • Interior Release: If possible, we unlock the car door first and use the interior trunk release.
  • Key Generation: If the release is disabled, we can cut a new key on the spot to open the trunk manually.
  • Lock Picking: Our skilled technicians can pick the trunk lock cylinder directly.

Why Call a Professional?

Trying to pry open a trunk can cause severe damage to the latch, hinges, and paint. Our technicians use professional tools and techniques to open your trunk safely and efficiently. We are available 24/7 for emergency trunk lockouts.
