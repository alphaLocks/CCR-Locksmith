Apartment & Condo Lockout Services

Fast, reliable access for tenants and property managers.

Getting locked out of your apartment or condo can be stressful, especially if you don't have a spare key or access to building management. CCR Locksmith provides 24/7 emergency lockout services specifically designed for multi-unit residential buildings.

Our Apartment Lockout Solutions

  • Non-Destructive Entry: We use specialized tools to unlock doors without damaging the lock or door frame.
  • Key Replacement: If you've lost your keys, we can cut new ones on the spot.
  • Lock Rekeying: For security, we can rekey your lock so old keys no longer work.
  • Master Key Systems: We assist property managers with master key system maintenance and lockouts.

Why Choose CCR Locksmith?

We understand the unique challenges of apartment and condo security. Our technicians are trained to work with a variety of residential locks, including deadbolts, knob locks, and electronic access systems. We arrive quickly, verify residency to ensure security, and get you back inside safely.

For Tenants

Locked out? Call us immediately. We'll need proof of residency (ID with address or lease agreement) to open the door.

For Property Managers

Partner with us for reliable locksmith services for your building. We handle lockouts, rekeying between tenants, and master key systems.
