Business & Office Lockout Service

Fast, reliable access when you're locked out of your workplace.

Time is money, and being locked out of your office or business means lost productivity and potential revenue. Whether you left your keys inside, the lock is jammed, or a key broke in the cylinder, CCR Locksmith provides 24/7 emergency business lockout services. We arrive quickly to get you and your employees back to work with minimal disruption.

We Unlock More Than Just Front Doors

Our commercial locksmiths are equipped to handle all types of office lockouts:

  • Exterior Doors: Storefronts, glass doors, and rear exits.
  • Interior Offices: Private offices, conference rooms, and supply closets.
  • File Cabinets & Desks: Lost keys to important storage units? We can open them and replace the locks.
  • Server Rooms: High-security access for IT infrastructure.
  • Storage Units & Warehouses: Padlocks and roll-up door locks.

Non-Destructive Entry Methods

We understand that you don't want your property damaged. Our technicians use specialized picking tools and bypass techniques to open locks without drilling or breaking them whenever possible. This saves you the cost of replacing expensive commercial hardware.

Emergency Rekeying

If your lockout is due to lost or stolen keys, we can immediately rekey your locks on-site. This ensures that the missing keys can no longer be used to gain unauthorized access to your business.

Don't let a lockout stop your business. Call CCR Locksmith now for immediate assistance.
