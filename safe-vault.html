Safe & Vault Services

Professional protection for your most valuable assets.

Safes are essential for protecting cash, documents, jewelry, and other valuables from theft and fire. However, when a safe malfunctions or you lose the combination, it can become a major source of stress. CCR Locksmith offers comprehensive safe and vault services for both residential and commercial clients. Whether you need a new safe installed, an old one opened, or a combination changed, our certified technicians have the skills and tools to handle it.

Our Safe Services

We cover all aspects of safe security and maintenance:

Safe Opening & Lockout Service

Locked out of your safe? Don't panic, and don't try to force it open. Our experts use advanced diagnostic tools and precision techniques to open safes without damaging the contents. In many cases, we can open the safe without destroying the lock, allowing it to be used again.

Safe Installation & Bolting

A safe that isn't secured is just a heavy box that a burglar can carry away. We provide professional installation services, including bolting your safe to the floor or wall to ensure it stays exactly where you put it.

Combination Changing & Repair

If your safe's dial is sticking, the electronic keypad is unresponsive, or you simply want to change the combination for security reasons, we can help. We service both mechanical and electronic locks from major manufacturers.

Why Hire a Professional Safe Technician?

Safes are complex mechanical or electronic devices designed to resist tampering. Attempting to repair or open a safe yourself can result in permanent damage, making it even harder (and more expensive) to open. Our technicians are trained in the latest safe manipulation and drilling techniques to ensure a successful outcome with minimal disruption.

Protect your valuables with confidence. Contact CCR Locksmith for all your safe and vault needs.
