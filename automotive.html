About

  • About Our Company

    Since 2015, Car Commercial Residential Emergency Locksmith Service, LLC has delivered fast, professional locksmith services across Southfield and Metro Detroit. Fully mobile, licensed, and insured, we handle car key replacement and programming, home rekeys and lock changes, commercial master key systems and access control, and 24/7 emergency lockouts. Expect honest, upfront pricing, clean workmanship, and a clear ETA. Every job is backed by our industry-leading 5-Year Warranty on parts and labor. Serving Oakland, Wayne, and Macomb counties within ~35 miles of Southfield. Need help now? Call (313) 889-3266. We install smart locks and high-security cylinders, repair ignitions, provide local dispatch with transparent quotes and no hidden fees.
