Secure Communication & Entry

Buzzer and intercom systems are essential for managing visitor access in multi-tenant buildings, offices, and gated properties. CCR Locksmith installs reliable intercom systems that allow you to verify identity before granting entry. From simple audio buzzers to advanced video intercoms with smartphone integration, we have the right solution for you.

Our technicians handle the entire installation process, including wiring, panel mounting, and integration with electric strikes or magnetic locks. We ensure clear audio and video quality so you always know who is at the door.

Our Intercom Services:

  • Audio Intercoms: Clear two-way voice communication.
  • Video Intercoms: See and hear visitors for enhanced security.
  • Multi-Tenant Systems: Directory-based systems for apartment complexes.
  • IP/Network Intercoms: Answer the door from your smartphone or computer.
  • Repair & Maintenance: Troubleshooting existing buzzer systems.

System Features

  • Remote Release: Unlock the door with the push of a button.
  • Night Vision: Clear video even in low-light conditions.
  • Vandal Resistant: Durable panels designed to withstand abuse.
  • Scalable: Easily add more units as your building grows.
