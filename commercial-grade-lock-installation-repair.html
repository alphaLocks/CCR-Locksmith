Commercial Grade Lock Installation & Repair

Heavy-duty security solutions for high-traffic business environments.

Business doors endure significantly more wear and tear than residential doors. They are opened and closed hundreds of times a day, often with force. That's why standard residential locks simply aren't enough. CCR Locksmith specializes in commercial-grade lock installation and repair, ensuring your business is secured with hardware built to last.

Understanding Commercial Lock Grades

We install ANSI/BHMA certified locks to ensure durability and security:

  • Grade 1: The highest level of security and durability. Designed for heavy-duty commercial use (schools, hospitals, public buildings). Tested to withstand 1 million cycles.
  • Grade 2: Intermediate security. Suitable for light commercial and heavy-duty residential use (offices, apartments). Tested to withstand 400,000 cycles.
  • Grade 3: Basic residential security. Not recommended for commercial exterior doors.

Our Commercial Lock Services

We provide comprehensive lock solutions tailored to your business needs:

  • Mortise Locks: High-strength locks integrated into the door edge, offering superior protection against forced entry.
  • Cylindrical Lever Locks: ADA-compliant lever handles commonly used in offices and commercial buildings.
  • Rim Cylinders & Deadbolts: Additional security layers for glass doors and rear exits.
  • Keyless Entry Locks: Electronic push-button locks for convenient access control without keys.

Repair vs. Replacement

Not every broken lock needs to be replaced. Our technicians can often repair commercial locks by replacing worn springs, latches, or cylinders, saving you money. However, if a lock is obsolete or compromised, we will recommend a high-quality replacement that meets current fire and safety codes.
