Safe Opening & Lockout Service

Regain access to your valuables without destroying your safe.

There are few things more frustrating than being locked out of your own safe. Whether you've lost the combination, the electronic lock has failed, or the key has broken off, CCR Locksmith is here to help. Our skilled technicians specialize in safe opening and lockout services, using advanced techniques to get you back in quickly and securely.

Common Reasons for Safe Lockouts

We handle all types of safe lockout scenarios:

  • Lost Combination: It happens to the best of us. We can often retrieve or reset the combination.
  • Mechanical Failure: Internal components can wear out or jam over time, preventing the safe from opening even with the correct code.
  • Electronic Lock Malfunction: Dead batteries, keypad failures, or wiring issues can lock you out of digital safes.
  • Broken Keys: If a key snaps off in the lock, we can extract it and cut a new one.

Our Safe Opening Methods

We prioritize non-destructive entry methods whenever possible. Our goal is to open your safe while preserving its functionality and security.

  • Manipulation: For mechanical dial safes, our experts can often determine the combination by listening to and feeling the internal mechanism. This causes zero damage to the safe.
  • Drilling: If manipulation isn't possible, we use precision drilling at specific points to bypass the lock. We then repair the drill hole professionally, leaving the safe secure and usable.
  • Scoping: We may insert a small borescope to view the internal mechanism and align the tumblers.
