Reliable Electric Door Strikes

An electric strike replaces the standard strike faceplate on your door frame, allowing the door to be unlocked remotely via an access control system. CCR Locksmith specializes in the installation and repair of electric strikes for all types of commercial doors, including aluminum storefronts, hollow metal doors, and wood frames.

Electric strikes are the backbone of many buzzer and card reader systems. We ensure your strike is properly rated for your door's fire code requirements and usage frequency. Whether you need a fail-safe or fail-secure configuration, our technicians have the expertise to get it right.

Our Services Include:

  • New Installation: Cutting and fitting frames for new electric strikes.
  • Replacement: Upgrading old or broken strikes with heavy-duty models.
  • System Integration: Connecting strikes to intercoms, keypads, and receivers.
  • Fail-Safe/Fail-Secure: Configuring locks to meet safety and security needs.
  • Panic Bar Integration: Installing strikes compatible with exit devices.

Why Use Electric Strikes?

  • Convenience: Unlock doors without leaving your desk.
  • Security: Keep doors locked while allowing controlled entry.
  • Durability: Designed for high-traffic commercial environments.
  • Versatility: Works with your existing door hardware (knobs/levers).
