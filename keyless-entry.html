Keyless Entry & Keypad Lock Installation

Experience the freedom and security of going keyless.

Tired of carrying bulky keychains or worrying about getting locked out? Keyless entry systems offer a modern, secure, and convenient alternative to traditional locks. CCR Locksmith provides professional installation and programming of keypad locks, touchscreen deadbolts, and biometric systems for homes across the region.

Types of Keyless Entry Systems

We offer a variety of keyless solutions to match your preferences and security needs:

  • Electronic Keypads: Simple and reliable. Enter a PIN code to unlock your door. Great for families with children.
  • Touchscreen Deadbolts: Sleek and modern designs that light up when touched. Many models integrate with smart home hubs.
  • Biometric Locks: Use your fingerprint for the ultimate personalized security. No codes to remember, no keys to lose.
  • Bluetooth/App-Enabled Locks: Unlock your door automatically as you approach with your smartphone.

Benefits of Going Keyless

Switching to a keyless system offers numerous advantages:

  • Convenience: Never worry about forgetting your keys again.
  • Enhanced Security: Eliminate the risk of lost or stolen keys. No lock picking or bumping.
  • Easy Access Sharing: Provide temporary codes for guests, housekeepers, or contractors, and delete them when no longer needed.
  • Audit Trails: Some advanced systems allow you to see who entered your home and when.
  • Auto-Lock Features: Set your door to lock automatically after a set period, ensuring your home is always secure.

Why Choose CCR Locksmith?

Installing a keyless entry system involves more than just screwing it onto the door. Proper alignment is critical for the motorized bolt to function smoothly and battery life to be maximized. Our technicians are experts in:

  • Professional Installation: We ensure the lock is fitted perfectly and the door is aligned correctly.
  • Programming & Setup: We help you set your master codes and user codes, and show you how to manage them.
  • Brand Expertise: We work with trusted brands like Schlage, Kwikset, Yale, and Emtek to provide durable and reliable products.

Upgrade your home's access today. Contact CCR Locksmith to explore our keyless entry options and find the perfect fit for your home.
