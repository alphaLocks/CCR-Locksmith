High-Security Maglocks

Magnetic locks (maglocks) use powerful electromagnets to keep doors securely locked. They are a popular choice for commercial buildings, glass doors, and emergency exits because they have no moving parts and are incredibly durable. CCR Locksmith installs and services a wide range of magnetic locks, from standard 600lb holding force models to heavy-duty 1200lb locks.

Maglocks are ideal for integrating with access control systems like card readers, keypads, and motion sensors. Our technicians ensure that your installation complies with all fire and safety codes, including the necessary release buttons and motion sensors for safe egress.

Our Maglock Services:

  • Single Door Maglocks: Standard installation for securing one door.
  • Double Door Maglocks: Dual magnets for double doors without a center mullion.
  • Glass Door Kits: Specialized brackets for frameless glass doors.
  • Shear Locks: Concealed magnetic locks for a cleaner aesthetic.
  • Request-to-Exit Devices: Motion sensors and push buttons for safe exit.

Advantages of Maglocks

  • Durability: No moving parts to wear out or jam.
  • Strength: Holding forces up to 1200lbs or more.
  • Fail-Safe: Automatically unlocks during a power outage (required for fire safety).
  • Versatility: Can be installed on almost any type of door frame.
