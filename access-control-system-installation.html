Modern Access Control Solutions

In today's security landscape, controlling who enters your facility is crucial. CCR Locksmith specializes in the installation and maintenance of comprehensive access control systems for businesses of all sizes. Whether you need a simple keypad for a single door or a networked system for an entire building, we have the expertise to design and install the perfect solution.

We work with leading brands to provide reliable, user-friendly systems that enhance security without disrupting workflow. Our technicians are trained in the latest technologies, ensuring your installation is code-compliant and fully integrated with your existing security infrastructure.

Our Access Control Services Include:

  • Keypad Entry Systems: Simple, code-based access for restricted areas.
  • Card Reader Systems: Proximity cards and fobs for employee access.
  • Biometric Scanners: Fingerprint and retina scanners for high-security zones.
  • Cloud-Based Management: Manage permissions remotely from any device.
  • Intercom Integration: Combine access control with audio/video verification.

Why Choose Access Control?

  • Enhanced Security: Eliminate the risk of lost or copied keys.
  • Audit Trails: Track who enters and exits your building and when.
  • Easy Management: Instantly add or remove user access privileges.
  • Remote Access: Unlock doors for deliveries or visitors remotely.
