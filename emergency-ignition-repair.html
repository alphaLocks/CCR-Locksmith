Emergency Ignition Repair

Mobile ignition services to get you back on the road.

There are few things more frustrating than getting into your car, inserting the key, and finding that it won't turn. Ignition problems can leave you stranded in parking lots, driveways, or on the side of the road. CCR Locksmith offers emergency mobile ignition repair. We come to your location to diagnose and fix the problem on the spot.

Common Ignition Problems We Fix

  • Key Won't Turn: Often caused by worn wafers inside the ignition cylinder or a worn key.
  • Key Stuck in Ignition: If you can't remove your key, the ignition might be damaged or the steering wheel lock might be binding.
  • Key Broke in Ignition: We can extract the broken piece and repair any damage to the cylinder.
  • Ignition Switch Failure: Electrical issues preventing the car from starting even if the key turns.

Repair vs. Replacement

In many cases, we can repair your existing ignition by replacing the internal wafers or springs, allowing you to keep your current key. If the damage is severe, we can replace the entire ignition cylinder and rekey it to match your door locks, or provide new keys for the new ignition.

Why Call a Mobile Locksmith?

Towing your car to a dealership or mechanic for ignition repair is expensive and time-consuming. Our mobile service saves you the tow truck fee and gets the job done faster, often at a lower cost than the dealer.
