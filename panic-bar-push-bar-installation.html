Panic Bar & Push Bar Installation

Ensure fire code compliance and building safety with professional exit device installation.

Panic bars (also known as push bars or crash bars) are a critical safety requirement for many commercial buildings, schools, and hospitals. They allow for rapid, unhindered exit during an emergency. CCR Locksmith specializes in the installation of high-quality, code-compliant panic hardware that meets all local fire and safety regulations.

Our Installation Services

  • New Construction: Installing panic devices on new doors for retail, office, and industrial spaces.
  • Retrofitting: Upgrading existing doors with modern, compliant push bars.
  • Alarmed Exit Devices: Installing panic bars with integrated alarms to prevent unauthorized use of emergency exits.
  • Vertical Rod Systems: Installing surface or concealed vertical rod devices for double doors.

Why Compliance Matters

Improperly installed exit devices can lead to failed fire inspections and, more importantly, can endanger lives. Our technicians are knowledgeable about life safety codes and will ensure your panic bars are installed at the correct height and function flawlessly.
