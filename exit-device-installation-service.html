Exit Device Installation & Service

Professional solutions for all types of commercial exit hardware.

Exit devices are the backbone of commercial door security and safety. From simple push pads to complex electrified hardware, CCR Locksmith provides expert installation and service for all major brands. We ensure your hardware is durable, compliant, and suited to your specific traffic needs.

Types of Exit Devices We Service

  • Rim Exit Devices: Surface-mounted hardware, ideal for single doors.
  • Mortise Exit Devices: Integrated into the door for higher security and durability.
  • Surface Vertical Rod (SVR): Latches at the top and bottom of the door frame for double doors.
  • Concealed Vertical Rod (CVR): Rods are hidden inside the door for a cleaner look and better protection.

Maintenance & Upgrades

Regular maintenance extends the life of your exit devices. We offer inspection and adjustment services to keep your doors operating smoothly. We can also upgrade your existing hardware to include features like electric latch retraction for access control integration.
