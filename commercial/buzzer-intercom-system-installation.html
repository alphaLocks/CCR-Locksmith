How Installation Works

  1. Site Survey & Quote: Evaluate doors, wiring routes, power, and tenant flow.
  2. Hardware Selection: Choose audio/video intercom, door release (strike/maglock), and power supply.
  3. Wiring & Mounting: Low-voltage cabling, panel install, camera alignment, network (if needed).
  4. Door Integration: Wire to electric strike or maglock; coordinate with closers and access control.
  5. Programming & Training: Directory setup, app enrollment, tenant instructions.
  6. Testing & Warranty: Live tests for talk/video/unlock; 5-Year parts & labor warranty.
