Commercial Safe Opening & Lockout in Metro Detroit

Non-destructive safe opening for cash, depository, TL-rated & office fire safes — 24/7 mobile response.

When a business safe won’t open—forgotten combo, keypad failure, time delay issues, lost keys, or damaged locks—our safe technicians use professional manipulation and diagnostic methods to open it cleanly and restore secure operation.

Call (313) 889-3266

  • Non-destructive methods first (manipulation/scoping)
  • TL-15/TL-30, depository & office fire safes
  • Licensed & insured · 5-Year Warranty

What We Open

  • Cash/deposit (drop) safes & night depositories
  • Office fire safes (mechanical dial or electronic keypad)
  • High-security safes (TL-15 / TL-30)
  • Pharmacy, retail, restaurant & back-office safes
  • Keyed safe locks, redundant locks, time-delay units

We verify business authorization/ID before any opening for everyone’s security.

Our Opening Process

  1. Assess & Verify: Confirm authorization; diagnose lock/bolt work, keypad, and relockers.
  2. Manipulation/Scoping: Attempt non-destructive opening techniques first.
  3. Precision Drill (If Needed): Minimal, surgical access to defeat failure points; we repair and refinish.
  4. Restore & Secure: Change combo, service/replace lock, test bolts/hinges, provide documentation.

  • Warehouses & light industrial
  • Multi-site chains & franchise locations

After-Opening Services

  • Combination changing (mechanical & electronic), keypad replacement
  • Safe lock upgrades (UL-rated locks, audit-capable electronic locks)
  • Bolt work service, hinge/door alignment, relocker inspection

FAQs

How fast can you open our safe?

Most commercial safe lockouts are resolved on the first visit. Time depends on failure type, safe rating, and lock model. We’ll provide an on-site ETA after assessment.

Our keypad is dead. Can you still open it?

Yes. We diagnose power/keypad faults and can open the safe using safe-techniques, then replace or upgrade the electronic lock if desired.

Will the safe look damaged afterwards?

We prioritize non-destructive entry. If precision drilling is required, we repair the access point, restore security integrity, and refinish discreetly.

Do you offer after-hours service?

Yes—24/7 mobile response across Metro Detroit for businesses that need access before opening hours or during emergencies.
