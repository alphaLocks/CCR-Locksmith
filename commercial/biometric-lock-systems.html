Biometric Lock Systems for Metro Detroit Businesses

Fingerprint & facial recognition entry — integrated with your strikes, maglocks, and access control.

Biometric access removes keys and cards from the equation. We design and install commercial-grade fingerprint and facial readers, connect them to your electric strikes or maglocks, and set up software for user enrollment and audit trails. One vendor, end-to-end.

What We Install

  • Fingerprint readers (capacitive/optical) with anti-spoofing
  • Facial recognition terminals with liveness detection
  • Select palm/vein readers for high-security environments
  • Biometric smart locks for interior offices & IT rooms
  • Controllers, power supplies, REX, door contacts & cabling
  • Integrations with electric strikes, maglocks, keypads & intercoms

All hardware is commercial-grade and configured with secure backup methods (PINs/cards) for ADA, gloves, or injury scenarios.

Where Biometrics Make Sense

  • Medical & dental suites (controlled records/med rooms)
  • Fitness centers & member-only facilities
  • Manufacturing/warehouse doors & cages
  • Schools & labs (restricted areas, IT closets)
  • Professional offices (law, finance, HR & file rooms)
  • Multi-tenant entries with audit requirements

How Our Biometric Installations Work

  1. Site Walk & Quote: We confirm door types, power, cabling paths, fire/life-safety requirements, and provide an upfront estimate.
  2. Hardware & Wiring: We mount readers, wire controllers and power, and tie into strikes or maglocks with proper fail-safe/fail-secure logic.
  3. Enroll & Train: We enroll staff, configure schedules/permissions, and train managers on adding/removing users and running reports.
  4. Test & Warranty: We test entry/egress, REX, alarms, and backups. All work carries our 5-Year Warranty on parts and labor.

Security, Privacy & Best Practices

  • Use encrypted templates (not raw images) where supported; limit data retention to what’s necessary.
  • Provide at least one non-biometric fallback (PIN/card) per door for accessibility and business continuity.
  • UPS backup on controllers and appropriate door hardware behavior during power loss (code-compliant).
  • Role-based permissions with time schedules; immediate revocation for terminated users.
  • Documented audit trails; periodic credential hygiene and firmware updates.

FAQs

Can biometrics replace keys and cards completely?

Yes, but we still configure a secure fallback (PIN or card) for accessibility, gloves, or temporary users. This avoids lockouts without weakening security.

How many users can we enroll and can we track entries?

Modern controllers support hundreds to thousands of users. We enable logging so you can review who entered which door and when.

Will doors unlock during a fire alarm or power outage?

We wire systems to meet life-safety codes. With proper power supplies/UPS and fail-safe/fail-secure selection, doors behave correctly during alarms or outages.

Do you integrate with our existing door hardware?

Yes. We routinely integrate readers with existing electric strikes, maglocks, and intercom/buzzer systems to preserve your investment and reduce downtime.

Do you service Metro Detroit businesses after installation?

Yes. We offer support, re-training, firmware updates, and expansions. All installs include a 5-Year Warranty on parts and labor.
