Panic Bar & Push Bar Repair for Metro Detroit Businesses

Emergency exit devices fixed fast — kept safe, smooth, and code-compliant.

When a panic bar fails, your people and your compliance are at risk. We repair rim and vertical-rod exit devices, alarmed push bars, door alignment and strike issues, dogging failures, and more — same day, across Metro Detroit.

Common Panic Bar Problems We Fix

  • Bar depresses but latch won’t retract (sticking or broken mechanism)
  • Door won’t latch or re-latches weakly (misaligned strike or bent latch)
  • Vertical-rod devices not engaging top/bottom latches
  • Alarmed exit bar won’t arm/disarm or false-alarms
  • Dogging feature stuck, missing, or stripped
  • Loose, broken end caps, covers, or trim; return spring failures
  • Closers and hinges causing egress or re-latch issues

We keep exits safe to use and easy to inspect — minimizing downtime and liability.

Our Repair Process

  1. Assess & Quote: Verify device type/brand, diagnose the fault, provide an upfront estimate.
  2. Precision Repair: Rebuild or replace worn parts, re-align strikes/rods, set dogging as needed.
  3. Function & Safety Check: Confirm smooth egress, proper re-latch, and compatible door closer settings.
  4. Compliance-Ready: Ensure signage, egress action, and hardware function are inspection-friendly.

If a device is beyond repair, we supply and install compatible replacements to restore compliance quickly.

FAQs

How fast can you repair a failed panic bar?

Same-day service is standard, and we provide 24/7 emergency response when an exit is unsafe or non-functional.

The door slams or won’t re-latch — is that the bar or the closer?

Often both need attention. We adjust/repair the exit device and the door closer/hinges so the door latches smoothly and safely.

Can you add exterior key access to an exit door?

Yes. We can provide compatible exterior trim/key cylinders and correct strikes so staff can re-enter without defeating egress.

Will you replace devices that can’t be repaired?

Yes. We carry compatible hardware and will install replacements that meet your door type, usage, and compliance needs.

Do you guarantee your work?

Absolutely. All workmanship is backed by our 5-Year Warranty. Hardware warranties vary by manufacturer.
